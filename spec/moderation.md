# moderation.md
## Verdict – Relationship Jury
### Moderation & Safety Specification v1.0

# Goals

- Keep platform legally safe
- Prevent harassment
- Prevent doxxing
- Minimize moderation costs
- Maintain trust in verdicts

# Moderation Philosophy

Verdict judges situations.

Verdict does not judge people.

Allowed:

"My wife became upset because I attended a wedding alone."

Not Allowed:

"My wife Anna Smith from Yerevan is crazy."
